About The Tour

The TD Bank 5 Boro Bike Tour isn’t just an endurance event—it’s a celebration of community, determination, and purpose. Ride alongside thousands of passionate cyclists across all five boroughs of NYC and become part of a movement that turns challenges into opportunities for growth and renewal.

Your Impact

The Next Ride (TNR) is the cycling heartbeat of The Next Step. As part of our team, every dollar you raise goes directly toward advanced prosthetics, specialized rehabilitation, and vital emotional support for Israeli amputees. This means:

State-of-the-Art Prosthetics

Restoring mobility and independence.

Specialized Rehabilitation

Offering physical and emotional support.

A Community of Care

Empowering Israeli amputees to face the future with courage.

Together, we turn every mile into a legacy of hope. Let’s ride from NYC to New Beginnings and prove that when we come together, there are no limits.
